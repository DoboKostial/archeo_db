This form creates/defines the evidence of photos in DB table tab_photos. The basix evidence item is scanned photo with references to terrain entities.

There are 3 sections on this page: Section upload for file upload and referencies definition, Photos gallery with overview of already uploaded photos and Stats and filter for quick querying uploaded photos.

  • Files: (required) is a button for uploading photos. Enables more files to upload (= they have the same attributes).
  • Photo type: (required) there are several photo types: 'vertical', 'horizontal', 'skew', 'general', 'detail'.
  • Author: (required) author of the photo.
  • Date: date of a photo creation.
  • Notes: any kind of additional information.
  • Links: section to define references to terrain/docu entities

Tips

  • Use -- bulk actions -- to reassign referencies/attributes of more photos.
