This form creates a new stratigraphic unit (SU) in tab_sj and its type-specific table (tab_sj_deposit, tab_sj_negativ, or tab_sj_structure).

Polygons

You can attach the SU to one or more excavation polygons. Start typing a polygon name, pick it from suggestions and click Add.

Stratigraphic relations
  • > means: current SU is above the referenced SU.
  • < meas: current SU is below the referenced SU.
  • = means: units are equal (same context).
Attach graphic documentation

Media upload is independent from creating a new SU. Select an existing SU first, then upload photos, sketches, drawings or photograms. Uploaded files are stored on filesystem and linked in M:N tables.
