This part creates/defines the evidence of drawings in DB table tab_drawings. The basix evidence item is probably terrain drawing as scan of paper drawing from the field. There is a Section 2 showing drawings already existed with short description. 3. section enables filtering of existing drawings according their attributes.

There are 3 sections on this page: Upload drawings. for file upload and referencies definition, Drawings gallery with overview of already uploaded drawings and Stats and filter for quick querying uploaded drawings.

  • Files: (required) is a button for uploading drawings. Enables more files to upload (= they have the same attributes).
  • Author: (required) author of the sketch.
  • Date: Date of a drawing creation.
  • Notes: any kind of additional information.
  • Links: Section to define references to terrain/docu entities

Tips

  • Use -- bulk actions -- to reassign referencies/attributes of more drawings.
