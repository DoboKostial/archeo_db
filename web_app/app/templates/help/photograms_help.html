This form creates/defines the evidence of photograms in DB table tab_photograms. The basix evidence item is digital photogram with references to terrain entities.

There are 3 sections on this page: Section upload for file upload and referencies definition, Photograms gallery with overview of already uploaded photograms and Stats and filter for quick querying uploaded photograms.

  • Files: (required) is a button for uploading photograms. Enables more files to upload (= they have the same attributes).
  • Photogram type: (required) there are several photo types: 'stereo', resection', 'synthetic', 'other'.
  • Author: (required) author of the photogram.
  • Date: date of a photogram creation.
  • Notes: any kind of additional information.
  • Links: section to define references to terrain/docu entities

Tips

  • Use -- bulk actions -- to reassign referencies/attributes of more photograms.
