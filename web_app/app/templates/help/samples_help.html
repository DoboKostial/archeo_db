This form creates/defines the evidence of samples in DB table tab_samples. The basix evidence item is probably the bag/sack as a container of one sample from one SU.

  • Sample ID: (required) is a unique identifier (primary key).
  • Type: (required) defines content of bag - use dropodwn list and optionaly define your own type of material.
  • SU: (required) defines ID of stratigraphic unit where samples were collected from.
  • Polygon: Sometimes You want to record of excavation polygon where sample was taken. FK to tab_polygons
  • Geopt: Sometimes its usual (healthy) to measure and store the precise position of sample. This field is FK to tab_geopts.
  • Description: any kind of useful information You would to store along.

Tips

  • There is a "Print label" button in list of samples bellow. Use it for printing paper labels intended to clue on bag with samples.
