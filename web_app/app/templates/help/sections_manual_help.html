This form creates/updates a section by defining ranges of measured point IDs from tab_geopts.

  • Section ID:: unique section/cut identifier (primary key).
  • Section type:: standard, cumulative, synthetic (see Figs. 01 - 03)
  • Point ranges:: any points definining section spatialy (FX, VB etc...) tab_geopts.
  • Cut stratigraphy units:: all SUs that are cut by section.

Tips

  • Point ranges are mandatory, but without points stored in Geopts table You are not able to build geometry.
  • Ranges may overlap; points are deduplicated by point ID during rebuild.
  • If geometry is not created, check that points exist in tab_geopts.
